Mount Everest is Earth's highest mountain above sea level

Mount Everest is located in the Mahalangur Himal sub-range of the Himalayas

The China-Nepal border runs across the summit point of Mount Everest

The elevation of Mount Everest is 8,848.86 m (29,031 ft 8+1⁄2 in)

The native names of Mount Everest are "Sagarmatha" in Nepali, "Chomolungma" in Standard Tibetan, and "Zhūmùlǎngmǎ Fēng" in Chinese

The first ascent of Mount Everest was made by Edmund Hillary and Tenzing Norgay on 29 May 1953
