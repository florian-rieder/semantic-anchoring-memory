The Goldman Sachs Group, Inc. is an American multinational investment bank and financial services company

The company was founded in 1869 by Marcus Goldman and Samuel Sachs

The headquarters of Goldman Sachs is located at 200 West Street, New York City, New York, U.S.

Key people at Goldman Sachs include Lloyd Blankfein (Senior Chairman) and David M. Solomon (Chairman and CEO)

The company offers services such as investment banking, global markets, asset management, wealth management, direct bank, mutual funds, and exchange-traded funds

The revenue of Goldman Sachs decreased to US$47.37 billion in 2022

The operating income decreased to US$13.48 billion in 2022

The net income decreased to US$11.26 billion in 2022

The total assets of Goldman Sachs decreased to US$1.44 trillion in 2022

The total equity of Goldman Sachs increased to US$117.2 billion in 2022

The number of employees at Goldman Sachs increased to 48,500 in 2022

The company has subsidiaries such as Goldman Sachs Capital Partners and Goldman Sachs Personal Financial Management

The capital ratio of Goldman Sachs is Tier 1 15.0% in 2022 according to Basel III Advanced

The company's ratings are Standard & Poor's: BBB+, Moody's: A2, and Fitch Ratings: A

Major Goldman Sachs offices are located in Jersey City, New Jersey, U.S. and Salt Lake City, Utah, U.S.
