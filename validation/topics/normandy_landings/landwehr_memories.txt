The Normandy landings, also known as D-Day, took place on June 6, 1944 in Normandy, France

The operation was part of Operation Overlord and the Western Front of World War II

The Allied victory resulted in five beachheads established in Normandy

Belligerents involved in the operation included the United Kingdom, United States, Canada, France, Australia, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Netherlands, Norway, New Zealand, Greece, South Africa, and Southern Rhodesia

Units involved in the operation included the United States First Army, Nazi Germany 5th Panzer Army, and Nazi Germany 7th Army
