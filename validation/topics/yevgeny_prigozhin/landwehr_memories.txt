Yevgeny Prigozhin was born on June 1, 1961 in Leningrad, Russian SFSR, Soviet Union

He died on August 23, 2023, in an airplane crash in Kuzhenkino, Tver Oblast, Russia

Yevgeny Prigozhin was known as "Putin's chef"

He was the leader of the Wagner Group from February 22, 2014, to August 23, 2023

Prigozhin was wanted by the FBI for electoral interference

He served a 12-year imprisonment from 1981 to 1990
